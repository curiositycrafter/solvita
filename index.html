What is SOLVITA?

A portable, all-in-one system integrating solar-powered clean energy, AI-driven health diagnostics, water purification, and offline emergency communication.

☀️

Solar Energy

Advanced solar panels provide reliable, clean energy even in the most challenging environments, ensuring continuous power for all system components.

💧

Water Purification

Multi-stage filtration system transforms contaminated water into safe, drinkable water, protecting communities from waterborne diseases.

💓

AI Health Diagnostics

AI-powered diagnostic tools provide immediate health assessments and monitoring, enabling early intervention and life-saving care.

📡

Emergency Communication

Offline-first communication system ensures connectivity during disasters, enabling coordination of relief efforts and emergency response.
